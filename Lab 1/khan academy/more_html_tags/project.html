Lasagna

  • Time: 2 hour
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Dough 1 pack
Meat 600 grams
Mayo 20 grams
Ketchup 20 grams
Wine 1 bottle

Step 1: Prepare meat

Step 2: Cook meat. Add wine to it when half-cooked

Step 3: Open dough

Step 4: Start assembling lasagna. First layer - dough, then meat, then sauces. Repeat until you run out ingridients

Step 5: Cook in the oven for 30 minutes at 150 degrees

Step 6: Enjoy!
